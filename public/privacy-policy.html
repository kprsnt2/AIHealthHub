Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2024

Welcome to AI Health Hub. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer: AI Health Hub is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

1. Information We Collect

AI Health Hub operates primarily on your device. We collect:

  • Health Information You Provide: Symptoms, health conditions, medications, and other health-related data you enter into the app.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent.
  • Language Preferences: Your preferred language setting (English or Telugu).

2. How We Store Your Data

All personal health data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This means:

  • Your health data stays on your device
  • We do not have access to your personal health information
  • Clearing your browser data will remove this information

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google's Gemini AI to provide health information and analysis. When you submit queries:

  • Your queries are sent to Google's servers for processing
  • Google's privacy policy applies to this data processing
  • We recommend not including personally identifiable information in your queries

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Local storage encryption (coming soon)
  • No server-side storage of personal health data

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data (via browser developer tools)
  • Delete your data (by clearing browser localStorage)
  • Export your data (feature coming soon)

6. Children's Privacy

AI Health Hub is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age without parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the application.
