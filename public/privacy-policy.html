Our Privacy Philosophy

We believe your health information is deeply personal and should remain under your complete control. That's why we built AI Health Hub with a privacy-first, offline-first architecture.

Why don't we collect your data?

  • Your health is personal - Medical information is among the most sensitive data. We have no business storing it.
  • No data = No breaches - We can't leak what we don't have. Your data never leaves your device.
  • Trust through transparency - You shouldn't have to trust us with your health data. So we designed the app to not require that trust.
  • You own your data - Export, backup, or delete your data anytime. It's yours.

What Data is Stored Locally

  • Health Profile (encrypted) - Age, gender, conditions, medications, allergies

  • Chat History (encrypted) - Conversations with the AI assistant
  • Drug Search History (not encrypted) - Names of drugs you've searched
  • Language Preference (not encrypted) - English or Telugu

Third-Party Services

When you use AI features, your queries (not your stored profile) are sent to Google's Gemini AI for processing:

  • Queries are sent over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • We recommend not including personally identifiable information in queries
  • Google's AI privacy policy applies to query processing
  • We do not store or log your queries
